Traveling to Charlotte

Airport: Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT)

Charlotte is American Airlines' largest hub on the East coast, so it's usually easy to find American flights from almost any city to Charlotte. CLT is also served by JetBlue, Southwest, United, Delta, and other airlines.

Getting Around

The hotels below and the wedding venue are all within a 30 minute drive from the Charlotte airport. Because it's economical and convenient for the area, we recommend you rent a car, but please let us know if you need alternate transportation arrangements.

Other convenient transportation options include the LYNX Light Rail (Hotels in Pineville below are closest to a light rail stop), which serves the South End and Uptown areas, as well as Charlotte's bus system, CATS.
