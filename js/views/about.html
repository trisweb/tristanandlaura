Our Story

How did this happen?

A few months after Tristan and Laura began dating, they took their first road trip together to Maine. While driving around, they stumbled upon a lighthouse a full mile into the middle of the harbor. They spent some time relaxing and taking in the views of the beautiful Maine seacoast, and realized this was a very special place.

Three years later, on their third visit to that special place, Tristan tricked Laura into posing for a picture with his new camera (for the 25th time that day). As Laura rolled her eyes and stood up, she saw a little black box instead of a camera, as Tristan opened the box (very carefully so as to drop the ring into the ocean) and proposed!

Laura swears she was surprised, although her perfect nails told a different story.

That day was just a formality in a relationship that was already solid as a diamond (ba-dum, chhhh). Over the years, Tristan and Laura have become a great team and they are so excited to officially tie the knot with all of you!
